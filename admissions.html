Admission Process: Step-by-Step

1

Inquire & Register

Begin by filling out our online inquiry form or contacting our admissions office. Register your interest for the upcoming academic year.

2

3

Successful applicants will receive an admission offer. Detailed instructions for enrollment will be provided.

6

Enrollment & Orientation

Complete the enrollment formalities and join our orientation program to prepare for the new academic year.

Eligibility Criteria

Admission to DreamBridge International School is based on a comprehensive assessment that evaluates the child's readiness for the respective grade level. Key factors include:

  • Age Requirement: Students must meet the minimum age requirement for their desired grade level as of [Date, e.g., June 1st] of the academic year.
  • Academic Proficiency: Demonstrated academic capability suitable for the curriculum, assessed through previous school records and our internal assessment.
  • Interview Performance: A positive and constructive interaction during the student and/or parent interview.
  • Previous School Records: Submission of academic transcripts and transfer certificates from previous institutions.
  • Behavioral Record: A record of positive behavior and conduct from previous schools.

Specific age cut-offs for each grade are available in our detailed admissions brochure.

Required Documents

To ensure a smooth admission process, please prepare the following documents:

  • 🗄 Duly filled and signed Application Form.
  • 🗎 Copy of Birth Certificate of the student.
  • 🗎 Copy of Aadhar Card / Passport of the student and parents.
  • 📜 Previous two years' academic reports / mark sheets.
  • 🎓 Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school (upon admission confirmation).
  • 📷 Recent passport-sized photographs of the student (4 copies).
  • 📷 Recent passport-sized photographs of each parent (1 copy each).
  • 🗄 Medical fitness certificate.

Admission FAQs

What is the age cut-off for Kindergarten admission?

For Kindergarten (Nursery), the child must be 3 years old by [Date, e.g., March 31st] of the academic year for which admission is sought. Specific age criteria for all grades are detailed in our admissions brochure.

Do you offer transportation facilities?

Yes, DreamBridge International School provides safe and reliable transportation services covering major routes. Details regarding routes and fees are available at the admissions office.

What is the teacher-student ratio?

We maintain an optimal teacher-student ratio to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. Typically, our ratio is [e.g., 1:15] in primary grades and [e.g., 1:20] in middle and high school.

Is an entrance exam compulsory for all grades?

For grades 1 and above, a diagnostic assessment is conducted to understand the child's academic readiness. For Kindergarten, it's typically an interactive session with the child.

Can we visit the school campus before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective parents to schedule a campus tour. You can book a visit through our website or by contacting the admissions office directly.

Scholarships Available

DreamBridge International School is committed to recognizing and nurturing talent. We offer a limited number of merit-based and need-based scholarships to deserving students each academic year.

  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: Awarded to students demonstrating outstanding academic performance.
  • Sports Scholarship: For students with exceptional talent and achievements in various sports disciplines.
  • Art & Culture Scholarship: Recognizing students with remarkable abilities in fine arts, performing arts, and music.
  • Need-Based Financial Aid: Available for families who demonstrate financial need, ensuring that quality education is accessible.

For detailed eligibility criteria, application procedures, and deadlines for scholarships, please download our Scholarship Policy document or contact our admissions team.
